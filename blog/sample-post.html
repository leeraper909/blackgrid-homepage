How BlackGrid Stops Heating Grant Scams in Real-Time

Published July 15, 2025

Heating grant scams target vulnerable people, especially the elderly and low-income families. These fraudsters send fake SMS and phishing emails promising grants that don’t exist.

BlackGrid uses patented AI and geofencing technology to identify and intercept these scams before they reach victims, shutting down the criminal campaigns at the source.

Our system’s zero-install defence layer works seamlessly across telecom and digital platforms, preventing fraudulent messages and calls in real-time without user disruption.

This protects the vulnerable, saves government funds, and keeps money circulating safely within the UK economy.
